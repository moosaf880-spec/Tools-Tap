Overview

Welcome to Tool Tap (\"we\", \"our\", \"us\"). We provide a collection of browser-based and server-assisted tools (the \"Services\") that help users convert, compress, edit and process files and text.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and the choices you have. We aim to minimize data handling and to respect user privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Files & Text you upload or paste

  • Most tools process files or text directly in your browser (client-side) and do not transmit your data to our servers.
  • If a tool requires server-side processing (e.g., for features that cannot run fully client-side), uploaded files are used only for the requested operation and are deleted as described below.

Usage & Technical Data

  • We may collect non-personal metadata such as browser type, device, operating system, and anonymous usage statistics to improve the Services.
  • We may use cookies or analytics tools to measure traffic and performance. These do not contain file contents.

2. How We Use Information

  • To perform the specific operation you requested (e.g., compress a PDF, convert a file).
  • To improve the Services, fix bugs, and analyze usage patterns.
  • To respond to support inquiries or enforce our Terms of Use, if necessary.

3. File Retention & Deletion

If a file is sent to our server for processing, we apply the following rules unless otherwise disclosed for a specific tool:

  • Temporary use: Files are stored only long enough to complete the requested operation.
  • Automatic deletion: Processed files are deleted automatically from our servers within a short period (for example, within 24 hours). We recommend you download processed files immediately.
  • Manual removal: If you need early deletion, contact us at the address below with details and we will attempt to remove the data.

4. Third-party services & libraries

We may use third-party libraries and services (for example, PDF processing libraries, analytics, or cloud storage for optional features). These libraries may run client-side and do not send your files off-site. When we use a server-side third party, we require contractual assurances about data handling. We do not sell your files or text to third parties.

5. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Use caution when uploading highly sensitive documents. If you need enterprise-grade confidentiality, consider using local tools or a dedicated secure workflow.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Tool Tap may use small browser cookies or analytics to measure site usage and performance. These are used for site improvements and do not capture file contents. You can disable cookies in your browser, though some site features may degrade.

7. Children

Our Services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you suspect such data was collected, contact us so we can remove it.

8. Your Choices & Rights
